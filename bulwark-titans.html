Team Overview

The Bulwark Titans are the first official team created in the VRChat Football League, established on Christmas Day, 2025. The team was founded by Nate Valesh, the leader of the Bulwark Empire, after members of his empire had been requesting a VR football team to represent them.

Team Details

  • Founded: December 25th, 2025
  • Head Coach: Nate Valesh
  • Status: Active
  • Stadium: The Titans Colosseum
